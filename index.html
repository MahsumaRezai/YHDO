Objectives US

Goal and Objectives of the training/ services: Intends to help adolescents and youths to organize their business idea and train them on business components that are essential for running a business. To train and develop capacities of micro businesses holders to overcome challenges and improve their operations. Support targeted micro and small businesses in promoting, developing, and sustaining growth. To identify, nurture and acquire new clients and opportunities for targeted micro businesses. Take part in Economic growth, employment generation as well as poverty alleviation in the targeted area.
